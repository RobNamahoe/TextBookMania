Textbook Mania

Textbook Mania allows students wishing to buy textbooks to connect to those students wishing to sell their textbooks. Make a profile, tell us what you need, then view a list of potential buyers and/or sellers. All of these options are easily accessible from our nav-bar!

Students

To register a new student, from the nav-bar, go to Students → Add New Student. You'll be prompted for the following:

  • Name (Required)
  • Email (Required and cannot already exist in our database)
  • Picture URL (Required and must be 100px by 100px)

Click Submit to add the student to the database. You'll be notified if your entry doesn't meet our requirements.

To see a list of all students in the database, from the nab-bar, go to Students → View Student List. From here all student information is visible. You're also able to edit and delete student profiles from here.

Textbooks

To register a new textbook, from the nav-bar, go to Textbooks &arr; Add New Textbook. You'll be prompted for the following:
  • ISBN-10 (Required and cannot already exist in our database)
  • Title (Required and cannot already exist in our database)
  • Author (Required)
  • Condition (Required)
  • Cover URL (Optional but will be automatically generated by us)

Click Submit to add the textbook to the database. You'll be notified if your entry doesn't meet our requirements.

To see a list of all textbooks in the database, from the nab-bar, go to Textbooks → View Textbook List. From here all textbook information is visible. You're also able to edit and delete textbooks from here.

Buy Orders

If you're looking to buy a textbook, let sellers know by creating a Buy Order. To create a Buy Order, from the nav-bar, go to Buy Orders &arr; Add New Order. You'll be prompted for the following:
  • The Buyers Email Address (Required)
  • ISBN (Required)
  • Offer (Required)

Click Bid to add the Buy Order to the database. You'll be notified if your entry doesn't meet our requirements. Any potential sellers will be listed on our Match page.

Sell Orders

If you've got a book to sell, let buyers know by creating a Sell Order. To create a Sell Order, from the nav-bar, go to Sell Orders &arr; Add New Order. You'll be prompted for the following:
  • The Sellers Email Address (Required)
  • ISBN (Required)
  • Offer (Required)

Click Sell to add the Sell Order to the database. You'll be notified if your entry doesn't meet our requirements. Any potential buyer will be listed on our Match page.

Matches

You've gone through the trouble of creating a profile and listing your orders now see what all that work was for. Click on Matches in the nav-bar and we'll find all potential buyers for each of your Sell Orders and all potential sellers for each of your buy Orders.
